|Platform| | |
|:-------|-|-|

|Ubuntu (16.04+) [x64]|[Stable](https://gpm.agchapman.com/stable/ubuntu/x64)|[Latest (unstable)](https://gpm.agchapman.com/unstable/ubuntu/x64)|

> [!TIP]
